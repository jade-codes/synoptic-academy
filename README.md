# Synoptic Academy

> Systems thinking education for underrepresented communities.

Synoptic Academy is a community interest company (CIC) based in Yorkshire/Hull, building accessible education for people who think non-linearly and were poorly served by traditional institutions.

We call them the Lost Girls and Boys. This is for them.

---

## What we do

- Teach systems thinking as a practical skill, not an academic abstraction
- Build learning experiences designed for non-linear minds
- Create community infrastructure for people who pattern-match across domains
- Make the kind of education that changes how you see the world, not just what you know
